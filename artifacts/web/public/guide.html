📦 How to Download Your Asset Manager Code

Do steps 1–2 in your Asset Manager project · Do step 3 back here in LRMC

1
In the Asset Manager project — click the ⋯ button in the Files panel

Look at the top of the left sidebar where your files are listed. You'll see a small button with three dots ··· — click it.

👆 The ··· button is highlighted in yellow/orange — it's at the top right of the Files panel
2
Click "Download as zip"

A small menu drops down. Click Download as zip — your browser will save a ZIP file to your Downloads folder.

3
Back in THIS project — upload the ZIP file

Switch back to this LRMC Replit project. Then drag the ZIP from your Downloads folder directly into the left file panel — or use the ··· menu here and choose "Upload file".
